EDA-Forbes World Richest Billionaire’s 2022

Project Details

Forbes world’s billionaire’s list is an annual ranking of the world’s biggest billionaire’s and their networth value cut across 4 major points; country, industry, source of income and philanthropy rating. The Forbes ranking is regarded to be one of the most popular rankings and the findings from her yearly analysis is largely accepted by lots of persons. Following the release of the 2022 list, i carried out an exploratory data analysis as a personal project, to get further insights on what the data holds.

Project insights

  • The Forbes list 2022 consisted of 2,668 Billionaires from 75 Countries.
  • Approximately 89 percent of billionaires are men while 11 percent are women.
  • Elon Musk is the richest man in the world with a networth of about $219,000B, and followed by followed by Jeff Bezos.
  • Among the top 10 countries, the USA has the most Billionaires — 725, followed by China — 539.
  • Following my analysis on the top 10 industries, Finance and Investment sector was seen to be the industry with the most number of Billionaires — 392, rightly followed by Technology — 343 and the least is Healthcare with 217 Billionaires.
  • From my analysis, i can say for sure that there is no relationnship between age and money. However, the majority of billioniare’s were seen to be aged between 50 & 80 years.
